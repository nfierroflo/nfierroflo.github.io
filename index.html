About me

I'm Nicolás Ignacio Fierro Flores, an Electrical Engineer and Data Scientist, among other things. I grew up in the northern part of the southern hemisphere, 1.7 km from the Tropic of Capricorn in Antofagasta, Chile, and pursued my higher education in Santiago.

I hold a Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Chile, where I focused my research on Robotics and Artificial Intelligence (AI), specifically in Multiple Extended Object Tracking (MEOT) utilizing Stereo Images. Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work across various sectors, including robotics, astroinformatics, healthcare, and e-commerce, in roles such as Robotics Software Engineer and Data Scientist. Eager to elevate my expertise, I aspire to pursue a Ph.D. program with a keen interest in applied Robotics and AI.

I earned my bachelor’s degree in Engineering Science in Electricity at the University of Chile. Additionally, I participated in a dual-degree program that will allow me to obtain an Engineering degree (M.Sc.) from École Centrale Supélec in Paris, France, in collaboration with the University of Chile. Both institutions equipped me with the necessary tools to gain extensive knowledge in the fields of robotics, machine learning, and software development.

I am dedicated to the constant pursuit of knowledge and continuous improvement in my field. My passion for technology and robotics drives me to keep exploring new frontiers in the world of engineering, robotics, and AI.
